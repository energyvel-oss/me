About Me

Robotics Developer & Engineer

I'm a passionate robotics developer with experience in designing, building, and programming autonomous robotic systems. My expertise spans across mechanical engineering, embedded systems, computer vision, and artificial intelligence.

My journey began with a fascination for how machines could interact with the physical world, which led me to pursue advanced degrees in robotics engineering. Throughout my career, I've worked on projects ranging from industrial automation to search and rescue robots.

I believe in creating robotic solutions that are not only technically sophisticated but also intuitive and accessible. My approach combines rigorous engineering principles with creative problem-solving to develop robots that make a meaningful impact.
